  Water Coloring Game

 

This game chooses a random picture from among your close relatives and converts it into a water coloring page. You can then select a color from the palette and paint with that color over the image. When you have finished painting the image, it will say the name of the person to whom that picture was attached.

Tap on the Next button to randomly choose a different picture.

Tap on the camera button to save a copy of your water coloring to your device, or to share it with your family by email or social media. Sharing or saving requires adult approval and it will ask you to enter your online family tree password before you can continue.
